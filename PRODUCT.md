# BE.RICH APP - Product Brief

## Product Purpose
Financial management dashboard for Brazilian users to track income, expenses, bank accounts, credit cards, bills, and investments in real-time with visual insights.

## Users
- **Primary**: Young professionals (25-45) in Brazil managing personal finances
- **Secondary**: Freelancers, entrepreneurs tracking business expenses
- **Context**: Desktop-first, used during work hours for monthly budgeting and analysis

## Brand Tone
- Modern, trustworthy, empowering
- Clear and direct (no jargon)
- Professional yet approachable
- Portuguese (pt-BR)

## Design Register
Product (design serves the dashboard functionality)

## Strategic Principles
1. **Clarity First**: Complex financial data made simple and scannable
2. **Trust**: Dark mode with subtle glassmorphism conveys sophistication
3. **Actionability**: Every metric should drive a decision
4. **Speed**: Quick month/year navigation, instant category filtering
5. **Accountability**: Visual progress bars and percentages for spending awareness

## Anti-References
- Cluttered bank interfaces with too many options
- Flat, emotionless financial dashboards
- Slow, laggy financial applications
- Hidden fees or unintuitive category systems

## Key Features
- Monthly dashboard with income/expense summary
- Category-based spending visualization (bar chart + progress bars)
- Bank account and credit card management
- Bill closure workflow
- Investment tracking with manual aporte entries
- Transaction history with category filtering
- Responsive design (desktop-focused)

## Color Palette Constraints
- Primary green: #3dba6a, #4caf50
- Secondary: #86efac (light green)
- Negative/Alert: #ef5350 (red)
- Dark background with glassmorphism effects
- All colors use OKLCH color space

## Typography
- Font family: System default (SF Pro Display, Segoe UI, Roboto)
- Hierarchy: H1 32px, H3 13px uppercase, body 14px
- Line length: Optimized for dashboard cards and tables
